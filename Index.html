How to play: 1 Player

Test your memory skills by choosing one of the 3 modes:

  • Warm Up (4 x 4 image grid)
  • Normal (6 x 6 image grid)
  • Ultimate Memory Test (8 x 8 image grid)

Open set of two images every turn while the clock is ticking. If the two images are same they stay open and you have two images less till the end.

Goal: Open all of the images in quickest time possible and show off to your friends.

How to play: 2 Players

Choose one of the 3 modes:

  • Warm Up Battle (4 x 4 image grid)
  • Normal Battle (6 x 6 image grid)
  • Ultimate Memory Battle (8 x 8 image grid)

The player open set of two images in his turn. If the two images are same they stay open, the player gets a point and plays one more turn.

Goal: Finish the game with more points than your oponent.
